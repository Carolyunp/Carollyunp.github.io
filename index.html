What we Offer

Our module provides a comprehensive exploration of two fundamental areas in mechanical engineering: elasticity and the finite element method (FEM). Delve into the principles of elasticity, including stress, strain, and constitutive relations, and understand how these foundational concepts form the basis for solving complex engineering problems. Building on this, the module covers spring and beam elements, offering a deep dive into FEM theory, real-world applications, and hands-on practice. Through interactive simulations and exercises, students will bridge theory and practice, mastering the tools and techniques needed to analyze and design advanced engineering systems.

Theory

This section provides you with the fundamentals of the finite element method. We start from the very basics and summarise the key concepts that you will need for your exam.

Interactive Simulations

Here you have the free reign to explore different configurations of spring and beam elements and see the effects of changing different inputs.
